Archetto

Bows for string instruments, cast in borosilicate glass. Each element carries the memory of four generations — luthier, inventor, lighting maker, artist. The sculpture moves in response to sound, its patterns tracing the invisible architecture of sound waves.

Chicago Athenaeum Good Design Award.

Supernova — Nuestros Recuerdos

A suspended field of light and mesh. Nuestros Recuerdos, our memories, is a collaboration between two artists separated by fourteen years and bound by the same family. The work explores how memory is not stored but recreated; each recollection a new construction, familiar and altered. Light projected through mesh generates cellular patterns that shift and dissolve, never the same twice. The unsaid, held in common. Worked through together.

In collaboration with Gabi Baldoni.

Mikko

A flexible mesh of aluminum and light, Mikko takes the form of Quetzalcóatl, the feathered serpent deity of Mesoamerican cosmology. Commissioned for the Sofitel Mexico City Reforma, it grounds a French sensibility in the ancient visual language of the land beneath it.
